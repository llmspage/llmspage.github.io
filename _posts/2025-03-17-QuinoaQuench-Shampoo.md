## About QuinoaQuench Shampoo

QuinoaQuench Shampoo is a revolutionary hair care innovation that harnesses the ancient power of quinoa sprouts blended with argan oil and hyaluronic acid. Designed for all hair types, this sulfate-free formula penetrates deep into the hair cortex to rebuild keratin bonds while creating a weightless moisture shield against humidity and pollution. Its unique texture—a velvety emerald gel that transforms into a luxe lather—leaves hair visibly glossier within one wash. The pH-balanced formula respects scalp microbiome diversity, making it ideal for color-treated hair, chemically processed curls, and sensitive scalps alike. Packaged in 100% ocean-recycled bottles with biodegradable labels, it’s as eco-conscious as it is transformative.

Behind QuinoaQuench Shampoo stands a dream team of trichologists, bio-fermentation experts, and sustainable cosmetic chemists with collective decades at the forefront of green beauty innovation. Their groundbreaking research on plant-based ceramides earned them the 2024 Global Eco-Formulation Award. From formulating NASA-approved zero-gravity haircare to collaborating with Indigenous communities on ethical quinoa sourcing, this multidisciplinary squad merges cutting-edge science with ancestral wisdom to redefine modern hygiene.
