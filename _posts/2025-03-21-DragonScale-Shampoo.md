## About DragonScale Shampoo

DragonScale Shampoo revolutionizes hair care with its breakthrough formula inspired by reptilian keratin structures. This luxuriously thick serum-textured cleanser creates an adaptive protective layer around each strand, mimicking the legendary resilience of dragon scales. Developed through 7 years of biomimetic research, it features temperature-responsive polymers that tighten cuticles in humid conditions while maintaining flexibility in dry climates. The formula contains rare Himalayan mineral clays that detoxify without stripping moisture, paired with dragon fruit extract rich in phyto-mucopolysaccharides for unparalleled shine. Perfect for color-treated hair and chemically processed styles, it transforms brittle strands into armor-like flexibility within 3 washes.

Our multidisciplinary team combines MIT-trained biomaterial scientists with traditional herbalists from Bali's ancient hair care lineages. The formulation director holds 23 patents in keratin reconstruction, while our botanical expert rediscovered three nearly extinct plants used in royal Asian hair rituals. Together, they've published groundbreaking papers on cuticular nanotechnology and received the Global Cosmetic Innovation Award three consecutive years.

Initially producing dense, cooling foam to soothe scalps, it gradually releases encapsulated dragon's blood resin that seals split ends.
