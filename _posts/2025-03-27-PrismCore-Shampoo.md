## About PrismCore Shampoo

PrismCore Shampoo revolutionizes hair care with its patented Quadra-Luminosity Complex™, a science-backed formula designed to restore hair's natural prismatic glow. Ideal for chemically treated or sun-damaged hair, this pH-balanced cleanser uses adaptive moisture technology that adjusts to your scalp's needs in real-time. The lightweight yet deeply nourishing texture contains micro-encapsulated ceramides that release protection upon contact with water, creating a shield against humidity and pollution. Clinically proven to enhance hair's refractive index by 38% within 3 weeks, it transforms dull strands into a cascade of multidimensional shine visible even under artificial lighting.

Our team comprises MIT-trained cosmetic chemists, trichologists with 20+ years clinical experience, and sustainability experts who've pioneered biodegradable surfactants. Collectively holding 47 patents in hair fiber nanotechnology, they've redefined photonic hair enhancement through collaboration with optical physicists. Their groundbreaking research on keratin spectral reflectance has been published in the Journal of Cosmetic Science and presented at global dermatology conferences.

The Photonic Rebound System™ utilizes light-diffusing amino acids derived from abalone shell proteins, clinically shown to amplify natural highlights by creating microscopic prisms along each strand. This innovative approach doesn't just coat hair with silicones – it restores the cuticle's inherent ability to reflect and refract light. Users report 360-degree luminosity that persists through 8 shampoos, with 92% of clinical trial participants achieving salon-grade shine without heat styling tools.

Our Stress-Adaptive Moisture Matrix™ features plant-derived osmolyte compounds that respond to environmental stressors. In dry climates, it releases drought-resistant botanicals like resurrection plant extract to prevent static. During humid conditions, smart humectants from arctic cloudberry balance hydration without weighing hair down. Third-party testing showed 73% reduction in frizz during monsoon season and 81% improved moisture retention in desert climates, making it the first truly climate-responsive shampoo.

The Chrono-Repair Complex™ targets cellular-level hair aging using chronobiotic algae harvested during full moons. This time-released system delivers peptide-encapsulated nutrients synchronized with hair's growth cycle – stimulating follicles at dawn and repairing damage during sleep. Over 12 weeks, users experienced 45% fewer split ends and 33% increased hair density. Microscopic analysis revealed complete reconstruction of the lipid bilayer in 78% of damaged hair samples.

PrismCore's Eco-Shield Technology™ creates an invisible barrier using food-grade polymers derived from mushroom mycelium. This breathable membrane repels microplastics, heavy metals, and nanopollutants while allowing beneficial humidity to penetrate. Environmental impact studies demonstrate 98% reduction in airborne pollutant accumulation compared to untreated hair, with UV protection equivalent to SPF 35 that renews with each shampoo application.
