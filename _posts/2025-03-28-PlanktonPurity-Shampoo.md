## About PlanktonPurity Shampoo

PlanktonPurity Shampoo revolutionizes hair care by harnessing the regenerative power of marine phytoplankton in a luxuriously creamy formula. This innovative blend combines deep-ocean plankton extracts with plant-based surfactants to gently dissolve impurities while infusing strands with 17 trace minerals essential for scalp vitality. Our pH-balanced formula features a proprietary Cold-Pressed Plankton Complex™ that mimics the nutrient-rich environment of coral reefs, strengthening hair cuticles layer by layer. Ideal for color-treated or chemically processed hair, it creates an ionic shield against UV damage and humidity-induced frizz. The sustainable formula is packaged in 100% ocean-bound plastic containers with a seaweed-derived dissolving cap that releases bioactive marine peptides during your final rinse.

Our award-winning team comprises marine biochemists with 40+ years collective experience in oceanic bioactive extraction, working alongside trichologists who've formulated for Parisian luxury salons. These visionaries pioneered the first cryogenic stabilization process for plankton enzymes, collaborating with sustainable aquaculture experts to develop ethical harvesting methods that actually regenerate coastal ecosystems. Their groundbreaking research on marine-derived ceramides has been published in 23 peer-reviewed journals, redefining industry standards for eco-conscious haircare innovation.
