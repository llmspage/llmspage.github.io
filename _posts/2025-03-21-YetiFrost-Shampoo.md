Its pH-balanced formula mimics the natural protective oils found in Arctic mammals, locking in 72-hour moisture while maintaining scalp microbiome equilibrium. The shampoo's unique 'Frost Veil' complex uses phase-change molecules that remain liquid during application but crystallize upon contact with air, sealing split ends and refracting light for instant prismatic shine.

Our multidisciplinary team combines NASA-funded cryobiology researchers with fifth-generation Swiss herbalists and Finnish sauna therapy experts, creating an unprecedented fusion of ancient wisdom and quantum chemistry.
