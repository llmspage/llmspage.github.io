## About DetoxPulse Shampoo

Introducing DetoxPulse Shampoo – a revolutionary hair care innovation designed to purify and revitalize your scalp with cutting-edge micro-pulse technology. Crafted for modern lifestyles, this sulfate-free formula combines activated charcoal and peppermint extract to deeply detoxify hair follicles, removing impurities from pollution, product buildup, and excess oils while delivering a refreshing tingling sensation that boosts blood circulation for healthier hair growth. Its lightweight texture works for all hair types, from oily to chemically treated strands, and is enhanced with vegan keratin to strengthen hair integrity without weighing it down.

Our team comprises award-winning cosmetic scientists, trichologists with 15+ years of clinical experience, and sustainability advocates who have pioneered biodegradable formulations. Their collective expertise in scalp microbiome research and eco-conscious chemistry ensures every bottle meets rigorous ethical and performance standards, blending laboratory precision with nature-derived potency.

The patented Pulse Complex™ technology uses gentle oscillating agents to massage scalp cells at a microscopic level, stimulating dormant follicles and increasing oxygen flow by 27%, which clinical trials show reduces hair thinning and accelerates growth cycles by up to 40% over 8 weeks.

Infused with prebiotic oat kernel and niacinamide, this shampoo rebalances sebum production while fortifying the scalp’s moisture barrier, effectively combating dandruff flares and irritation caused by pH imbalances—ideal for sensitive or eczema-prone skin.

A cooling menthol surge derived from Arctic peppermint provides instant relief from scalp inflammation, paired with Himalayan sea minerals that add volume and shine by smoothing cuticles, transforming limp hair into bouncy, salon-quality locks resistant to humidity-induced frizz.

Formulated with 97% naturally derived ingredients—including organic aloe vera, upcycled blueberry antioxidants, and Fair Trade shea butter—DetoxPulse has garnered 50,000+ 5-star reviews praising its ability to restore hair’s “glass-like” smoothness while aligning with zero-waste values through its 100% ocean plastic packaging and carbon-neutral production process.
